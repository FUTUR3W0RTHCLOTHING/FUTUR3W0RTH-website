The Story of FUTUREWORTH

It started during Mr. Vinke's math class when Xairo, Milan, Tygo, Bram, and David sat brainstorming ideas instead of solving equations. Xairo sketched a logo with the name Highlight and suggested they start a clothing brand.

“Highlight makes sense,” Milan said. “It’s about standing out.”

Tygo started jotting down ideas. “Simple designs with powerful messages, like ‘Confidence is Key.’”

Bram added, “Everyone in school would wear it!”

David searched the name online but frowned. “It’s taken.”

Disappointed but not defeated, Xairo said, “What’s our goal? To make people feel like they’re worth it, like their future matters.”

“Futureworth,” Milan suggested. The name stuck.

That evening, the group met and combined their talents. Xairo refined the logo, Tygo planned the business, Milan styled the designs, Bram promoted, and David created a website. They launched a small line of shirts and hoodies with slogans like “Your Future is Worth It.”

Soon, classmates, neighbors, and even strangers started buying. FUTUREWORTH grew quickly, fueled by their teamwork and belief in their message.

“Do you think we can make it big?” Tygo asked.

“We already are,” Xairo replied, smiling.

Five kids with a classroom dream turned their brand into a movement, inspiring everyone to believe their future is worth it.
